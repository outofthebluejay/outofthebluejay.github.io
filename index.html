Submit! [submissions open until November 17 2023, 11:59pm EST]

ZENIADA accepts previously unpublished poetry (including prose poetry) and artwork. Anyone who is enrolled in an undergraduate program for the current school year is welcome to submit.

Email your submissions to submit@zeniada.com.

  • The subject of your email should be either "Poetry" or "Art."
  • In the body of the email, include your: name, graduation year, school, and any notes regarding your work.
  • Poetry should be attached as a Word (or other editable) document. Do not include your name, school, or any other identification. Note: We take a maximum of 3 poems! Each poem and piece of art should be sent as a separate document.

Any questions regarding submissions can be sent to ask@zeniada.com.
